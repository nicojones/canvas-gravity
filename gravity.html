Canvas Experiment... #1

I wanted to learn the <canvas> HTML object and I decided to make some balls bounce around.

What can you do?

  • Change the settings: Bounce %, number of balls, gravity...
  • Close the screen, so balls bounce on the ceiling as well
  • Click anywhere on the screen to add a ball in a random direction
  • Click and drag anywhere on the screen to slingshot a ball. The longer you wait, the slower it gets slingshotted!
